# Equity Research Repository

This repository contains structured equity research reports and valuation models for publicly listed companies across various sectors and geographies. It is intended to support analysts, investors, and finance enthusiasts in conducting fundamental analysis and making informed investment decisions.

## Purpose

To centralize and share detailed equity research on companies, including:
- Financial statement analysis
- Valuation models (DCF, comparables, etc.)
- Investment theses and risk factors
- Sector and macroeconomic context
